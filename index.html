The Festival

Buds Fest

BUDS FEST is a festival curated by rapper Buds. Since its creation, four editions have been held: two at the iconic Coupole in Biel/Bienne (around 1,000 attendees each), one in São Paulo, Brazil (around 500 attendees), and, as of this year, an open-air edition at Gurzelen in Biel/Bienne welcoming approximately 2,000 people.

Bringing together artists from different countries, regions, and languages, BUDS FEST creates a unique space where cultures, sounds, and communities meet without boundaries. Known for its strong sense of community, family-like atmosphere, and cultural diversity, the festival has built a reputation for consistently filling venues and delivering unforgettable experiences.
